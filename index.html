Feature Overview

Explore all the powerful features ReWear offers

Donate Page

On this page, users can enter cloth details such as type, size, color, description, and image. They can choose how many clothes they want to donate. Upon submission, the clothes become available in the Browse section.

Browse Page

Here, users can see all available clothes with filters like size, gender, age group, location, etc. If they like any item, they can click "Request This Cloth" to initiate a request.
